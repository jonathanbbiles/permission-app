Permission — Support

a private place to come home to yourself

Permission is a private journal — write, speak, or draw a gentle daily reflection. Everything stays on your phone; nothing is ever collected or uploaded.

Need help?

Email jonathanbbiles@gmail.com and we'll get back to you.

Common questions

I forgot my passcode. For your privacy, your passcode is stored only on your device and can't be recovered. You can delete and reinstall the app to start fresh (this also erases your entries).

Where are my entries stored? Only on your iPhone — never on a server.

How do I save a copy of an entry? Open the entry and tap Export a copy to share it wherever you like.
